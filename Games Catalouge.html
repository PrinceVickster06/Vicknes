Dishonored 2 is a classic example of the “immersive sim” — a genre where detailed worldbuilding and consistent mechanics give players many paths to every goal. It’s set in a quasi-Victorian world called the Empire of the Isles, ruled by the young empress Emily Kaldwin with the help of her father and bodyguard Corvo Attano, protagonist of the first Dishonored.
